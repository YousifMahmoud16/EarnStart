Top Websites That Pay Without ID in 2025

Not everyone has access to government-issued ID, especially teens and people in certain countries. The good news? There are still websites where you can earn money online without going through identity checks.

Below is a list of trusted platforms that let you start working or earning with just an email address — no passport, no national ID, and no hassle.

1. Clickworker

Clickworker offers simple online tasks like writing short texts, categorizing images, or testing websites. Signing up is easy, and ID is not required unless you hit high payout thresholds.

Website: clickworker.com

2. SproutGigs

SproutGigs (formerly Picoworkers) is great for microtasks. You can earn by writing reviews, testing apps, or following social media accounts. Most users get paid without needing to verify their identity.

Website: sproutgigs.com

3. Freecash

This site is popular among students and beginners. You earn coins by answering surveys, installing apps, or playing games. You can cash out to PayPal, crypto, or gift cards — no ID needed in most cases.

Website: freecash.com

4. Earnably

Earnably rewards you for completing offers, surveys, and watching videos. It's beginner-friendly and pays in PayPal or gift cards. You can sign up and withdraw without uploading any ID.

Website: earnably.com

5. SuperPay.me

Another option for earning through paid surveys and short tasks. You can cash out to PayPal with very low limits, and many users report receiving payouts without needing to verify their identity.

Website: superpay.me

Note: Superpay.me is temporarily unavailable. According to their official website, the platform is expected to relaunch in Summer 2025. We’ll update this article as soon as it becomes active again

Important Notes

  • Some platforms may ask for ID if suspicious activity is detected or if you request large payouts.
  • To avoid issues, use your real email and be honest when filling out profiles.
  • If you're under 18, make sure to check each platform’s age requirement.

Final Thoughts

Getting started online doesn't always mean handing over personal documents. These platforms give you a chance to earn from home with just a device and internet connection. Start small, stay consistent, and scale as you go.
